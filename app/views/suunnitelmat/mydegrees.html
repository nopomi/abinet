Description

LUT's Master's programme in Computer Science emphasises the viewpoint of technology in IT. The aim of the programme is to prepare students both professionally and academically for the areas of information technology that offer extensive career opportunities.

The programme also provides students with the readiness to undertake postgraduate studies and independent studies in information technology. Graduates will be equipped to challenge the rapidly changing world of ICT through their capability to maintain a high level of expertise and knowledge. The Master's Programme in Computer Science leads to the degree of Master of Science (Technology).
